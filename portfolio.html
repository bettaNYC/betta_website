Financial Reporting System

At InnoTech, I designed and implemented a financial reporting system that provides real-time tracking and analysis of business expenses. This system delivers clear insights into costs, profitability, and financial impact across projects, enabling data-driven decision-making.

System Capabilities
  • Real-time expense tracking, consolidating all company expenditures.
  • Automated financial analysis to assess total costs, profit, and loss at the project level.
  • Detailed expense categorization to identify key cost drivers.
  • Actionable insights to optimize spending and enhance profitability.
Business Impact
  • Improved financial visibility, supporting strategic decision-making.
  • Identification of key cost areas to drive efficiency and cost reduction.
  • More accurate budget forecasting to sustain long-term operations.
  • Enhanced project-level profitability assessment for better financial planning.

By providing precise, data-backed financial insights, this system has strengthened our financial strategy, improved operational efficiency, and supported sustainable business growth.

Warehouse Analytics Dashboard

I developed a comprehensive method for warehouse analysis, identifying critical KPIs and creating an interactive dashboard using data extracted from SAP. This tool provides essential visibility into inventory movements, stock value, and operational efficiency metrics.

Methodology
  • Identified critical warehouse KPIs to monitor performance and efficiency
  • Extracted and consolidated data from SAP systems
  • Designed visualizations to track inventory movements and values
  • Created automated dashboard for real-time monitoring
Dashboard Components
  • Consumption analysis by product category and location
  • Warehouse value trends with monthly comparisons
  • Part number movement tracking with percentage analysis
  • Average lead time analysis by product type
  • Stock value assessment for moved vs. non-moved inventory
Business Impact
  • Enhanced visibility into warehouse performance and inventory status
  • Improved inventory turnover through identification of slow-moving items
  • Reduced carrying costs by optimizing stock levels
  • Data-driven decisions for procurement and warehouse management
